About MHCC

Montes HealthCare College (MHCC) began as the vision of brothers, Juan and Alejandro Montes. After 30 years of working in the medical field, the two doctors became inspired to form their own institution of higher learning. MHCC was thus established with the mission of helping students prepare for rewarding careers and enriching the community. Now, our college has become a life-changing experience for many of our alumni. Boasting three programs, each with comprehensive curricula in their respective fields, Montes HealthCare College is ready to help you shape your future.
